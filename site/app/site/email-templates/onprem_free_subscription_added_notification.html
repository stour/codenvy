Welcome to Codenvy

Thank you for activating Codenvy On-Prem.

Our On-Prem docs will help you with sizing, administration and integrating Codenvy with your internal systems, projects and teams. If you've installed On-Prem on a single node you can easily migrate to a multi-node configuration if needed.

Codenvy is based on the open-source Eclipse Che project. By creating your own extensions and plugins you can fully customize your On-Prem system. There are examples on the Eclipse Che site and docs on how to add new plugins to your On-Prem system.

By default Codenvy On-Prem sends user information back to Codenvy.com. Paid subscribers can turn off remote data push - email us if you'd like to discuss this option. Additionally, Codenvy offers professional services to help integrate our system with your tools and processes.

If you have any questions please visit our Support Forum
